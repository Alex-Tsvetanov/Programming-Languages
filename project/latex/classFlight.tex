\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a flight, including its schedule, route, and assigned plane. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classFlight}{Flight}} class encapsulates details about a specific flight, such as the takeoff time, route length, origin and destination airports, and the plane assigned to the flight. It also provides methods to calculate workload and flight duration.
